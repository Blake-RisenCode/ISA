The most helpful place on the internet for international students

We understand how difficult it can be for international students

We know that studying abroad can be a daunting and overwhelming experience, which is why we have created a supportive and welcoming space for you to connect with others and share your experiences. Our community is completely free to join and participate in, and we strive to create a trustworthy and reliable environment for you to feel comfortable in.

Our Promises to you

Find answers you are looking for

Promise #1

We are dedicated to providing resources, support, and guidance to help you succeed. Whether you need help finding housing, navigating academic challenges, or simply want to connect with like-minded individuals, our community is here to help.

Find international students like you

Promise #2

Our team has been through it all – from trying to communicate in a new language to navigating unfamiliar surroundings and working part-time to pay rent, which is why we’ve created a supportive online community for international students just like you.

Find focus and productivity

Promise #3

Our community is a fun and interactive way to work together, chat, or simply focus on your studies in a silent online environment. Give it a try and see how much you can accomplish!

Co-study online

We understand the challenges of finding productive hours to study, which is why we’ve created a space specifically designed to help you get the most out of your time online. Whether you’re looking for a place to collaborate with others, need a little extra motivation to stay focused, or simply need a reliable power source for your computer, our online co-working platform has you covered.
